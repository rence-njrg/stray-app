How it works

Tell us your route

Enter where you're driving. We find every gem within 15 km of your path — not a bounding box, but an actual corridor along your road.

Every place is scored for niche-ness

Anything with more than 200 Google reviews doesn't qualify. We re-check scores weekly — when a place gets popular, we flag it.

Curated by people, not algorithms

Every gem is reviewed by our team or a verified local strayer before it goes live. We'd rather have 200 great places than 2,000 average ones.
