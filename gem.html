Where fourteen streams converge in the middle of Aberdare Forest. The track in takes about 25 minutes from the tarmac — rocky in places, but most cars handle it. There's a clearing at the junction with enough space to park and sit by the water. On a weekday you'll probably have it to yourself. On weekends, occasionally one or two other groups. Nobody sells food out here.

Good to know

  • Bring your own food and water. No vendors, no signal.
  • The last 3 km of track floods in heavy rain. Check the weather before you go.
  • Arrive before 9am for the best light through the trees.
  • Entry is informal — no gate, no fee as of April 2025.
  • Colobus monkeys are frequently spotted on the track in. Don't feed them.
